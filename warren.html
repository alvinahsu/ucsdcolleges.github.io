Warren Toward a Life in Balance
The Earl Warren College motto, "Toward a Life in Balance," is inspired by the philosophy of our namesake, former Chief Justice Earl Warren. The College is dedicated to cultivating ethically responsible citizen scholars who will flourish during their careers at UC San Diego and beyond.
Enrollment(2020): 5,438 - 17.1% of total enrollment
There are a total of 4 general education requirements for Warren College: Warren Writing, Ethics and Society, Formal Skills, and Programs of Concentration (PofCs) or Area Studies. Warren Writing
A two-course sequence that must be taken at UCSD.
  • First: WCWP 10A
  • Second: WCWP 10B
All students must satisfy UC Entry Level Writing Requirement before enrolling in WCWP 10A. Must be take for a Letter Grade
Ethics and Society
A two-course sequence that must be taken at UCSD. Courses may not overlap with any other general-education requirement.
  • First: PHIL 27 or POLI 27
  • Second: PHIL 28 or POLI 28
You must complete the Warren Writing requirement before enrolling in either PHIL 27 or POLI 27.
Formal Skills
Two courses in calculus, symbolic logic, computer programming and/or statistics are required from the following list:
  • HDS 60 MATH 3C, 4C MATH 10A or 20A MATH 10B or 20B MATH 11, 18
  • PHIL 10, 12 POLI 30 or 30D, 5 or 5D (or Econ 5) PSYC 60, 70 SOCI 60
  • LIGN 17 COGS 3, 8, 14A, 14B COGS 18/CSE 7 CSE 3, 8A, 8B, 11
  • CSS 1, 2ECE 5, 15, 25 MAE 5 NANO/CENG 15
Courses may overlap with your major, minor, or other general education requirements.
Programs of Concentration (PofCs) or Area Studies
Students are required to complete two PofCs or Area Studiesoutside of your major discipline. They must represent all three disciplines:
  1. Humanities and Fine Arts
  2. Natural Sciences, Math, and Engineering
  3. Social Sciences
PofCs are required for Non-B.S. Engineering Majors Area Studies are required for B.S. Engineering Majors ONLY
